How to Play [DCG]

The Pack

[DCG] is played with two 52-cards decks (without Jokers).

Object of The Game

Get the other player's HP to 0

Card Actions

  • A - Sheild (can be played immidiatly after enemy attack).
  • 2 - Moves a card from the table to the player's own trash pile.
  • 3 - Deals 3 DP to the enemy.
  • 4 - Deals -5 HP to the enemy and -3 HP to the player.
  • 5 - Locks a card on the table.
  • 6 - Deals 5 DP to the enemy and -1 HP to the player each turn.
  • 7 - The player chooses a card to give to the enemy or to take from him,
    then the enemy chooses a card to take from the player or to give him, respectivly.
  • 8 - Removes the enemy's hand cap by 1.
  • 9 - Adds 1 to the player's hand cap.
  • 10 - Resurrects a card from the player's trash pile.
  • J - Heals the player by 1 HP each turn.
  • Q - Heals the player by 5 HP once.
  • K - Deals -2 HP for the enemy each turn.

Setup

  • Each player starts with a full deck.
  • Each player draws 5 cards.
  • The default hand cap is 5.
  • The default HP for each player is 20.

The Play

At the start of each turn, the player draws two cards from his deck, then chooses one to add to his hand. The other card returned to the bottom of the deck. The player keeps taking cards until they reached their hand cap.


After the player drew their cards, they can play one of them. The played card will be placed on the player's side on the table. the maximum amount of cards on the table for each player is 5. If the player have maximum amount of cards they must replace one of the cards to play another card (6 cannot be replaced). The replaced card is trashed.


When one of the player reaches 0 HP, he loses.
