How to Play [DCG]

The Pack

[DCG] is played with two 52-cards decks (without Jokers).

Object of The Game

Get the other player's HP to 0

Card Actions

  • A - Sheild (can be played immidiatly after enemy attack).
  • 2 - Moves a card from the table to the player's own trash pile.
  • 3 - Deals 3 DP to the enemy.
  • 4 - Deals -5 HP to the enemy and -3 HP to the player.
  • 5 - Locks a card on the table.
  • 6 - Deals 5 DP to the enemy and -1 HP to the player each turn.
  • 7 - The player chooses a card to give to the enemy or to take from him,
    then the enemy chooses a card to take from the player or to give him, respectivly.
  • 8 - Removes the enemy's hand cap by 1.
  • 9 - Adds 1 to the player's hand cap.
  • 10 - Resurrects a card from the player's trash pile.
  • J - Heals the player by 1 HP each turn.
  • Q - Heals the player by 5 HP once.
  • K - Deals -2 HP for the enemy each turn.
